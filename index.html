Social Media & Branding Strategy





"Bright Ideas Gone Agile" was published to officially open the new website, launched simultaneously as the brand new Instagram account I started for Agile Lab. By landing the start-up on Instagram, my goal was to give a voice to the team that made the company what it is, and attract new talent in Big Data. I developed a social media content strategy focused on employer branding, including a short IGTV series of interviews in vlog format that looked into the experiences of our team. We covered topics such as the most rewarding project they worked on, the reason for choosing Agile Lab for their personal and professional growth as new grads, and the benefits of working in an environment that constantly incentivizes its team to take up new challenges.

As I launched agilelab_official and developed the social media strategy from scratch, I saw the account go from having no content nor metrics to reaching an average of +36% new accounts per month. Specifically, I reached on average 55% of non-following accounts per post - hitting the peak of 82% impressions from non-followers with my most successful post. To increase reach and engagement on a steady basis, I created a dashboard on Tableau to identify potential trends and visualize any data correlations among my posts



Back Market | SEO Italy





Developed the SEO strategy for the Italian website from scratch: built topic clusters around our target keywords and created buying guides accordingly; peformed a low-hanging fruit study for the optimization of SEO texts and taglines; optimized our top LPs following in-depth keyword analyses, consistently increasing our organic keywords and managing to rank in the top 3 for the refurbished keyword category for all our best-selling products. Tracked SEO visibility against our SEO competitors, monitoring the evolution of keyword visibility per category as compared to our competing websites.

Tracked SEO KPIs, reaching a weekly increase in SEO sessions of +30% on average as compared to last year, and a +47% increase in organic sessions as compared to the previous semester. Exceeded my SEO targets of the quarterly projected goals for both SEO sessions (+16%) and SEO GMV (+20%)

Identified 5 relevant categories to track: refurbished, price, used, renewed and golden. Managed to rank in the top 3 for the refurbished category and in the top 10 for the golden category of all best-selling iPhones; the biggest accomplishment was getting the golden keyword of iPhone 11 to shift from position 22 to position 6 (one of the most competitive keywords for iPhone, with an average annual search volume of 295k according to Ahrefs)
